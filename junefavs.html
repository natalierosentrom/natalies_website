Portal 2

You guys. Portal changed my life I'm not even kidding. I think everyone should play it if they can. It is one of brother Trevor’s favorite games and I’ve always wanted to play but it wasn’t until he let me use his old razor computer that I could play on it bc lord knows my little Macbook could NOT handle it. I played Portal and then immediately went on to Portal 2.

You GUYSSSS UGH. the game design. The graphics were beautiful for one. The game felt so huge? Like it was actually so immersive. And the puzzles were so fun! I got slightly addicted, i will not lie. i did become a bit rotten and neglectful to everything around me and was just playing portal for hours straight. Its ALL i wanted to do. Just be in a dark room with Computer and play.
This was the first actual video game I've played on PC and it was so awesome. I loved the narrative and the twists! I was gagged multiple times. I laughed, I cried, I gamed. 10/10
